How to use the app

  1. 1

    Browse what’s on offer

    Explore available items and surprise bags from cafés, restaurants and shops near you — all in one place.

  2. 2

    Choose & order

    Add what you want to your basket and place your order — pick the bag or items that suit you.

  3. 3

    Delivery or pickup

    At checkout, choose whether you’d like your order delivered or ready for collection — whatever fits your day.

  4. 4

    Pay securely

    Complete payment in the app with your preferred method — quick, simple and protected.

  5. 5

    Confirmation, code & QR

    Right after payment you’ll receive an order confirmation with a unique code and QR in the app. Show it at pickup or use it to track your delivery — then enjoy your food.
